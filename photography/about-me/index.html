About Me

Hello — I'm Jackee.

I'm Jiaqi Jiang (Jackee Martinez), an NPL NSW accredit photographer based in Sydney.

I'm providing my works to UNSW FC, where I follow the club through its fixture.

My work spans five areas I keep coming back to: bird photography, aviation photography, sports photography, news photography, and portraiture. Different subjects, but the same question underneath — what does a moment look like when you hold it a breath longer than it wanted to stay?
